Our Story

Crafting Beautiful Ceilings
Since 2010

Arham Traders was founded with one clear purpose — to bring world-class false ceiling craftsmanship to homeowners and businesses at honest, competitive prices. What started as a small local workshop has grown into a trusted name with over 500 completed projects.

We specialize in gypsum, POP, grid, and PVC ceilings — offering complete end-to-end services including design consultation, material supply, skilled installation, and post-project support. Our team of 40+ certified craftsmen brings years of experience to every single project.

Service 01

Gypsum False Ceiling

Gypsum false ceilings are the most popular choice for modern homes and offices. Lightweight, durable, and fire-resistant, gypsum boards allow for endless design possibilities — from simple flat ceilings to intricate coffered patterns, curves, and cove lighting effects.

Our certified team ensures perfect board alignment, seamless joint filling, and a flawless Birla White putty finish. We handle all wiring integration for LED strip lights, spotlights, and chandeliers.

Fire-resistant & moisture-resistant boards
Excellent sound insulation properties
Smooth finish, ready to paint any color
Integrated LED cove & recessed lighting
Ideal for living rooms, bedrooms & offices
5-year warranty on workmanship

POP Ceiling Design

Plaster of Paris (POP) ceilings combine timeless artistry with structural flexibility. POP is an excellent medium for intricate moldings, curved cornices, dome ceilings, and custom decorative elements that bring personality and grandeur to any space.

Our experienced POP artisans handcraft each design with precision — from simple borders and medallions to elaborate ceiling roses and multi-level tray designs with ambient lighting integration.

Fully customizable shapes & patterns
Decorative moldings, cornices & domes
Seamless, crack-free smooth finish
Suitable for high-ceiling spaces
Long-lasting with proper sealing
Cost-effective for ornate designs

Service 03

Grid Ceiling

Grid ceilings (also called suspended or T-bar ceilings) are the go-to choice for offices, retail stores, hospitals, and commercial spaces. The modular tile system allows easy access to HVAC, electrical, and plumbing above the ceiling — essential for commercial builds.

We supply and install Armstrong, Gyproc, and other premium grid ceiling systems with mineral fiber or gypsum tiles, offering excellent acoustic performance and clean aesthetics.

Quick and clean installation
Easy panel replacement without damage
Superior acoustic dampening
Fire-rated tiles available
Compatible with all lighting types
Ideal for large commercial areas

Service 04

PVC Ceiling

PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) ceiling panels are the smartest choice for moisture-prone areas such as kitchens, bathrooms, balconies, and garages. They are 100% waterproof, termite-proof, and require virtually zero maintenance — making them both practical and cost-effective.

Available in hundreds of colors, textures, and patterns — including wood grain, marble, and gloss finishes — PVC ceilings allow you to create a stylish look even in utility spaces.

100% waterproof & moisture-resistant
Termite-proof & pest-resistant
Easy to clean — just wipe down
Wide range of colors & textures
Very affordable option
Fast installation, minimal dust

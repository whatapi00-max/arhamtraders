FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a false ceiling cost?
The cost varies depending on the ceiling type, design complexity, and project area. As a general guide: Gypsum: ₹80–₹160/sqft, POP: ₹60–₹120/sqft, Grid: ₹50–₹90/sqft, PVC: ₹45–₹80/sqft. Contact us for a detailed, accurate quote specific to your project.
How long does installation take?
For a standard home bedroom (200 sqft), installation typically takes 2–3 days. Larger areas or complex designs may take 4–7 days. We always provide a confirmed timeline before starting your project.
Do you offer a warranty on your work?
Yes, absolutely. Every project completed by Arham Traders comes with a 5-year workmanship warranty. If any issues arise due to our installation within this period, we fix it at zero cost to you.
Is a site visit really free?
Yes — 100% free. Our expert will visit your site, take measurements, understand your requirements, and share design recommendations. There's no charge and no obligation to proceed.
Which ceiling type is best for my home?
It depends on your space and requirements. Gypsum is ideal for living rooms/bedrooms for a premium look. POP suits decorative designs. PVC is best for kitchens/bathrooms (waterproof). Grid is perfect for commercial spaces. Our team will advise you based on your specific needs.
